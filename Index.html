About Rock Inu

Rock Inu is not just another meme token — it's a community-powered cryptocurrency built for the future. It combines fun, utility, and decentralized technology to offer a truly unique experience for its holders.

Our tokenomics are designed to promote scarcity and liquidity growth. Every transaction burns 1% of the tokens and adds 2% to liquidity, encouraging long-term holding and stability.

Rock Inu is built with features like minting, pausing, and auto-liquidity — all governed by a transparent and community-first approach.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 100,000,000 ROCK
  • Burn on Each Transaction: 1%
  • Automatic Liquidity: 2% of each transaction
  • Features: Minting, Pausing, Community Governance
  • Ownership: Community Owned, No Pre-sale
